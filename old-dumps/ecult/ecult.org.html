Welcome to eCult!

eCult is Dav Clark's personal website. I write about things of greater ontological moment on SciDAV - my site for discussing science and somatics.

The title is ironic.  Initally, it served as a way of posting invites to things like "ritual brunch." Now it is mostly just me writing things, and testing out technology for more serious projects, such as my partnership with Jon Sandruck and an online holistic neuroscience community (although this is seriously stalled now that I'm actually doing neuroscience again).

I am too lazy right now to have a sidebar or other "modern" navigational system.  The truth is, this site is mostly for testing things I don't know how to do yet.  Things like page layout bore me (and I'm not very good at it).  Anyway, here are the thing(s) you may want to check out.
