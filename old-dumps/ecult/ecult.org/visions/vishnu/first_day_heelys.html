My First Day with Heelys

Publication date: Jun 21, 2005 3:39:38 PM

Heelys are so rad. I just got my first pair. A beginner style, no grind plate, but solid.

You'll probably want to check out this Totally Rad Video.

There is nothing you can do with Heelys that you probably couldn't do better on regular skates or a skate board. But Heelys have one huge advantage - stealth. No one can tell if you're sporting a pair. Maybe you walk a little funny, but lots of people walk funny.

So you can easily walk into a restaurant or a store or even your work and the guards ain't gonna notice. No one will notice, in fact,until you pick up your toes and start heeling skate (that's the official conjugation so far as I can tell).

Then it's totally like, "Dude, did you see that guy? Did he just float across the floor or something?"

Or, take for example, my first run-in with a security guard (in my building). I glide up to the door, but then (respectfully) walk like an ordinary mortal through the lobby. "You're gonna have to take off those rollerblades!" commands the guard. But I stand in front of him, looking down.

"I'm not wearing rollerblades."

And he looks, and I'm not. And he can think of nothing to do besides laugh as I walk onto the elevator.

Subversive whimsy, my friends, is what I'm all about. And Heelys are totally my official footwear.
