Our color-coordinated arm-warmers are now complete. The five members of The Home at 1845 Adam Clayton Powell, Jr. Blvd. are finally ready. Like the Voltron team, once united our party will be unstoppable.

Past parties are already the stuff of legend - leaving party-goers begging for more of the same. So come prepare to share magical performances, dance like a fiend or simply soak up the spirits. But this time the myths will be even more real.

For your partying pleasure, we'll be mining the richly spiced media of India's epic present. We'll be packing in all the Bollywood, Bhangra and assorted Indian delights we can squeeze into a 2500 square foot apartment.

Simultaneously on a diferent level of reality, we'll be observing a black occasion. We will be celebrating the 40th birthday of our very own Tom Davis.

Unlike Voltron, and I mean to be quite literal when I say this, we won't be waiting until the end to pull out the blazing sword.
