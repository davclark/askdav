Could It Be I'm Falling in Love?

Publication date: Mar 29, 2005 7:11:25 PM

And here I am, in this Turkish cell phone store, whose computers usually have a porn site already opened, and I read your message, and think of this scene in a telenovela about spanish harlem where this guy says goodbye to this woman, and the sun shines on their faces and the wind blows their hair, and there is this song, and he says, see you later. which is correct.
