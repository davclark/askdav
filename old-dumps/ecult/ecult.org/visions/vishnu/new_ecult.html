Towards a new eCult

Publication date: Mar 18, 2005 4:13:36 PM

I have more or less decided that Zope is the way to go. And, of all the content management suites out there for Zope, Silva seems to have the best feature set for future proof, interoperability and so on.

As time goes on, the site should become more and more exactly what you want. If you want to just check in every once in a while, you can still just surf to ecult.org. If you'd rather be e-mailed as soon as I announce a party in the New York area, but otherwise only recieve at most one e-mail every two weeks indicating new content, that should be possible too. Everything will be available via RSS and suchlike.

So, please let me know what you think, what you want and how much you're willing to pay!
