Recordings

Recordings of ATMs are here for free on ZumoDrive. if you join ZumoDrive, you can stream these files to your phone and stuff in addition to downloading them. If you use my "join ZumoDrive" link, I get some bonus referral space - do it!

These are probably NOT the best recording of any particular lesson (unless it's one I made up). But they can be great for review, or as an intro / example if you are curious about my teaching. I welcome feedback - critical or appreciative!

The .mov/.m4a files were recorded with the FLAC codec using apple quicktime. You can download the player here if you don't have it. In the future, I'll hopefully transcode things to a more lightweight codec (i.e., the files will get a lot smaller). But for now, I'm doing the minimum to get things done!
